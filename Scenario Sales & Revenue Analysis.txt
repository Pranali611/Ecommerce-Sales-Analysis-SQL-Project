Scenario: Sales & Revenue Analysis
Company: A retail company wants to analyze its sales performance across different regions, products, and time periods. They have given you a dataset containing sales transactions and customer details.

Problem Statement:
Analyze the total revenue generated per month.

Identify the top 5 best-selling products.

Find the total sales made by each salesperson.

Calculate the average order value per customer.

Identify which region has the highest revenue.

Find customers who have made more than 3 purchases.

Determine the most profitable product category.
